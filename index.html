Call of Duty: Mobile is a 2019 first-person shooter video game developed by TiMi Studio Group and published by Activision for Android and iOS. Released as a free-to-play title,
it was one of the largest mobile game launches in history, generating over US$480 million with 270 million downloads within a year.
Call of Duty: Mobile was published in other regions by Garena, Tencent Games, VNG Corporation, and TiMi Studio Group.
